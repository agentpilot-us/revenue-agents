1. Core Architecture
AEs do two things: reach out proactively (company activities) and respond to signals (target account events). Both are triggers into one content engine.



The content generation engine (Content tab → Email Copy by Buying Group) already exists. This spec adds: trigger context injection, expanded channels, buying group persona filtering, Exa.ai as the signal detection and enrichment layer, and a customization model for per-rep tuning.

4. Exa.ai Integration Layer
Exa provides three integration surfaces that map directly to AgentPilot’s needs. Exa is not just a search engine — it’s the intelligence backbone that detects signals, enriches contacts, and monitors target accounts continuously.
4.1 Exa MCP — Real-Time Search & Research
Used for on-demand searches triggered by the AE or the NBA engine.


4.2 Exa Websets — Automated Entity Monitoring
Used for continuous background monitoring of target accounts. This replaces/augments the firecrawl cron job with a more intelligent, criteria-aware monitoring system.

Webset per Target Account
When an AE adds a target company, AgentPilot creates an Exa Webset for that company with searches and enrichments:
Search: “Leadership changes at [Company]” with entity_type: “people” → detects New C-Suite, VP Hire signals
Search: “[Company] hiring surge job postings” with entity_type: “article” → detects Hiring Surge, Job Posting signals
Search: “[Company] funding acquisition expansion” with entity_type: “news” → detects Funding, M&A, Geo Expansion signals
Search: “[Company] layoffs restructuring” with entity_type: “news” → detects Layoffs, Exec Departure signals
Monitor: Cron “0 6 * * *” (daily at 6 AM) re-runs all searches and flags new items as signal triggers

Enrichments for Signal Context
Each Webset item gets enriched to provide the AE with actionable context:
Person name + title + LinkedIn URL (for leadership signals)
Previous company + role (for background research)
Signal category classification (text enrichment: “Classify: new_c_suite | new_vp_hire | exec_departure | other”)
Relevance score (number enrichment: 1–10 based on signal priority and division match)
4.3 Exa Company Research Skill — Account Intelligence
Used to populate and refresh the Account Research section on Target Company pages. Replaces the current firecrawl-based research with richer, AI-structured output.

How It Maps to Your Company Data Page
Your Company Data page (images 1–4) already has: Value Propositions, Product Capabilities, Use Cases, Target Personas, Differentiators, etc. The Exa Company Research skill produces the equivalent intelligence for each target account:
Company basics → employees, HQ, revenue (replaces current AI Generated basic research)
Business overview → products, markets, strategy (feeds Overview tab)
Recent news → feeds Signals tab with pre-classified signal types
Tech stack → informs division-level product fit matching
Competitors → informs competitive battlecard content generation

Integration Pattern
Exa MCP endpoint: https://mcp.exa.ai/mcp?tools=web_search_advanced_exa,company_research_exa,people_search_exa,crawling_exa
Websets MCP endpoint: https://websetsmcp.exa.ai/mcp?exaApiKey=YOUR_KEY
Both connect as MCP servers. The Chat Agent (bottom-right in image 2) can call Exa tools directly. The backend cron job uses the Websets API for scheduled monitoring.

6. Content Generation Engine
6.1 Current State
Content tab: Division dropdown → generates Email Copy with Subject + Body. Copy, Send to Contact, Regenerate. Uses Your Company Data to personalize.
6.2 Three Additions
A. Trigger Context Banner
Above the division dropdown. Shows the active trigger context injected into the AI prompt:
Signal: “New VP of Engineering hired at GM” → Vehicle Engineering & Simulation
Activity: “NVIDIA DRIVE v2.0 Launch” → All divisions with DRIVE use case
Passed via URL param: ?trigger=<triggerId>. If no trigger, banner hidden, works as today.
B. Channel Selector

C. Buying Group Persona Filter
Multi-select for seniority. Filters which contacts appear in Send list AND adjusts AI tone:
C-Suite only: Strategic, ROI-focused, board-ready language
VP and above: Executive but with tactical hooks
Director and above: Implementation-focused, feature-specific
All contacts: Practical, user-centric
Default set by trigger’s personaLevelDefault. ‘New C-Suite Executive’ defaults to C-Suite only. ‘Workshop/Training’ defaults to All contacts.
7. Customization Model
7.1 Template-Level Customization
Every built-in trigger template is a starting point. The AE customizes at three levels:
Enable/disable: Toggle which of the 59 templates are active for them
Priority override: Change priority (e.g. make ‘Geographic Expansion’ Critical instead of Medium)
Action override: Edit the 5-step action sequence to match their sales motion
7.2 Custom Triggers
AEs can create entirely new trigger types not in the built-in catalog:
Custom Signal: “Competitor mentioned in earnings call” — AE defines the Exa search query, priority, actions, timing
Custom Activity: “Annual customer appreciation week” — AE defines the activity type, target segments, content template
Custom triggers are stored as TriggerTemplate records with isBuiltIn: false. They get the same matching, content generation, and tracking as built-in triggers.

9. Sales Map Configuration & Onboarding
9.1 Sales Map New Sections
Signal Preferences
Card per signal type from 4 categories. ON/OFF toggle + priority selector. Expandable to see/edit the 5-step action sequence. Default state: intelligent defaults based on sales motion.
Activity Templates
Card per activity type from 7 categories. AE enables the ones their company actually does. Connected Google Sheet shows as a feed of upcoming activities.
9.2 Onboarding Flow (New Steps)
After the AE sets their objective and adds target accounts:

Step 1: “What signals should we watch for?”
System shows all 21 signal types grouped by category with recommended defaults
AE toggles ON the relevant ones. For enterprise expansion (like NVIDIA/GM), defaults: New C-Suite, VP Hire, Hiring Surge, Job Posting, Page View, Funding, Renewal Approaching = ON
System creates Exa Websets for each target account with searches matching enabled signals
AE can edit action steps for any signal template right here, or do it later
Step 2: “What does your company do to reach accounts?”
System shows all 38 activity types grouped by category
AE enables the ones their company actually does (events? research reports? gifts?)
If Your Company Data has Products/Events/Feature Releases, shows them: “Activate any of these now?”
Optional: connect a Google Sheet URL for team-managed campaigns
Each activated item shows preview: “NVIDIA DRIVE v2.0 Launch → will generate content for 3 divisions at GM”
Step 3: “Review your playbook”
Summary screen: X signals monitored, Y activities enabled, Z target accounts covered
AE can adjust priority, actions, or persona levels for any template
System starts Exa monitoring immediately. First signals may appear within hours.
10. End-to-End User Flows
10.1 Signal → Content (Reactive)
Exa Webset Monitor detects: “New VP of Engineering hired at GM” (daily 6 AM scan)
System creates Trigger: type=SIGNAL, category=new_vp_hire, priority=HIGH, matched to Vehicle Engineering division
Dashboard Hot Signals: Card appears with amber dot, division tag, 5-step action preview, timing countdown
AE clicks CTA: Deep-links to GM → Content tab with ?trigger=<id>&channel=linkedin_inmail
Content tab loads: Trigger banner + Division pre-selected + Channel = LinkedIn InMail + Persona = VP and above
AI generates: Uses trigger context (new VP, their background from Exa) + division data + company value props
AE copies InMail: Pastes into LinkedIn. Switches to Email, generates follow-up for Day 3. Trigger status → SENT.
10.2 Activity → Content (Proactive)
AE activates trigger: In Your Company Data, clicks “Announce” on NVIDIA DRIVE product (or Google Sheet row goes active)
System matches: Exa company_research confirms which divisions have DRIVE-relevant use cases. Creates Trigger per match.
Dashboard Recommended Plans: “NVIDIA DRIVE v2.0 → GM ADAS: Draft announcement” + “… GM Vehicle Eng: Draft announcement”
AE clicks: Content tab with trigger pre-loaded. Selects Email → VP and above. AI generates personalized DRIVE announcement for ADAS.
Multi-channel: AE switches to LinkedIn Post, generates a thought-leadership post about DRIVE for their own profile.
Multi-division: Switches division to Vehicle Engineering, generates different content anchored to simulation use case.
